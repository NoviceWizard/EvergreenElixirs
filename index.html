    About Evergreen Elixers and our mission statement

    Here at Evergreen we've been brewing honest, strong potions for over seventy years. Through pestilence and paltry sum, our humble establishment has kept to using our tried and true recipes.

    Seventy years ago our forefather made the mission statement of only brewing the strongest potions. We've dilegently upheld this oath which you can regard throughout our history in such events as...

    • The Magical Massacre of Craag An.
    • Sudden Mead Flood of Brugge.
    • The Healing Plauge of 1312.

    Through magic scroll and accute poltergist-ism we have fashioned a retelling of the conversation in which our founder began the idea of his mission through the market and promise to the people. Such can be viewed at the portal apparent upon thy screen.

    That being said no knights errant will be sold potions of a potency over their ranking.

    Each item we create falls under a defined category of elixir. Here you can browse which of our lines would best fit you.

    Oils

    These are elixirs we create with the purpose of pouring across one's blade to highten the damage output.

    02

    Potions

    These are one's typical elixirs which can span any number of magicks. Potions can be remedies to injury or ailment, one time spells, limited time betterment of the persons, and much more.

    03

    Ointments

    These are slow working versions of your common medicinal potion which more precisely target a given ailment. Elixirs such as these are better on those less accustom to potions.
